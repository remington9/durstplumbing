Who We Are

Harold's Plumbing Inc. has been owned and Operated by Harold Durst of San Antonio Texas Since 1982. Harold attended Robert E. Lee High School. Harold took an interest in plumbing as a Junior in High School . Harold graduated from Robert E. Le in 1972. Thanks to Rudy DeGarza and the vocational plumbing classes offered at the school Harold's future was formed. He saw a need for plumbing and knew plumbing would never go away. Plumbing is a good, honest, hard working profession that could always help others through his plumbing skills and knowledge.


Harold's Plumbing Corporation was established in 1982. We have been in business for 33 years. We have been proud to celebrate 33 years of success due to dedicated hard working employee's, loyal customers and contractors. Our company mainly engages in Commercial and Residential Plumbing. We carry a Texas State mandated Master Plumber's License and are fully insured.

The President is Harold Durst, Vice President is his son Armand Durst, Treasurer his wife Judith Durst, Secretaries & Bookers his daughters Brittany Durst and Tanya Tatar , Dispatcher his son Jimmy Tatar. We are a family owned and operated business dedicated to giving customers years of quality plumbing service now and in the future. We Service San Antonio, Austin, New Braunfels, Bulverde, and the surrounding area's.

Our Motto "Plumbing With Pride"

Our Licensed plumber's know that focusing on completing a job with "Pride" not only makes the customer satisfied , but the plumber will we proud of the job they have completed. This enables a long lasting relationship between the customer and our business. Harold realized as a young man "Plumbing With Pride" is what quality professional service is all about.
